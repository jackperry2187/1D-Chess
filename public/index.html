1D-Chess

Created by Jackson Perry

Playing the game:
You are playing white.
Rooks move forwards and backwards any number of spaces, without jumping.
Knights move 2 spaces forwards and backwards, and can jump over adjacent pieces.
Kings move 1 space forwards and backwards, without jumping.
After 1 second, black will respond to the move you played and the Game Status will update.
Your goal is to win!
Hit the "Reset Board" button to try again from the beginning!
